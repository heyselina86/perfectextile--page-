About This Fabric

What Is Ultrasonic Quilted Microfiber Fabric?

Ultrasonic quilted microfiber fabric is made by bonding or quilting polyester microfiber bedding fabric layers through ultrasonic technology or quilting processes. Compared with traditional stitched quilting, ultrasonic quilting creates clean, thread-free patterns with stable bonding and a smooth surface.

It is widely used for comforters, quilts, mattress protectors, bedspreads, pillow covers and padded home textile products — making it a core material choice for global bedding fabric applications.
