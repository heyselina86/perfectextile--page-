FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is polyester microfiber bedding fabric?–
Polyester microfiber bedding fabric is woven or knitted from ultra-fine polyester filament yarn, then brushed, dyed, printed or finished for bedding use. It offers a soft hand feel, stable color performance and consistent quality for bulk production of sheets, duvet covers, comforters and quilts.
What bedding fabric types does Perfectextile supply?–
We supply six core fabric types: dyed, printed, peach skin, embossed, jacquard, and ultrasonic & quilted microfiber fabric — each developed for different bedding applications, market positioning and price points.
Can you customize GSM, width, color and printing design?–
Yes. GSM, fabric width, dyed color, printing design, hand feel and finishing can all be customized to your specification. Share your target market and requirements, and our team will recommend suitable options.
