What Is Peach Skin Fabric?

Peach skin fabric is a soft woven polyester microfiber fabric that has been brushed to create a smooth, velvety hand feel with a subtle matte finish — similar to the soft outer surface of a peach.

For bedding and home textile applications, it offers excellent softness, durability, easy care and cost-effective bulk production value. The brushing process raises fine fibers on the surface, giving the fabric its characteristic lightweight warmth and comfortable feel.

Perfectextile supplies polyester peach skin fabric as grey fabric and finished fabric for bedding manufacturers, importers, wholesalers and home textile brands who require stable quality and flexible customization.
